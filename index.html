About the project and the team

 

The project
The purpose of this project is to create a digital product that makes a positive impact and increases awareness of crime related issues in Stockholm.
The 'crimezonesthlm' web visualises and maps crime reports provided by the Swedish Police in real time.
Meet the Team
We are motivated and data driven Hyper Island students who are committed to build upon today's technologies for a better tomorrow!
